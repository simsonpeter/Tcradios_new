How to Install on Android

Follow these simple steps to install the TC RADIOS Android app from the Google Play Store.

Step 1: Open Google Play

Tap the Get it on Google Play button above on your Android phone. It will open the TC RADIOS app page in the Play Store.

Step 2: Tap Install

On the Google Play page, tap Install and wait until the download and installation are complete.

Step 3: Open the App

After installation, tap Open in Google Play, or find TC RADIOS in your app drawer and start listening.

Step 4: Add Shortcut (Optional)

You can long-press the TC RADIOS icon in your app drawer and drag it to your home screen for quicker access.

How to Install the Web App (PWA)

Install TC RADIOS to your home screen on Android and iPhone for an app-like experience.

Android (Chrome)

1. Open the web app: tcradios-new.vercel.app or tap Open Web App above.
2. Tap the browser menu (three dots).
3. Tap Install app or Add to Home screen.
4. Confirm, and an icon will appear on your home screen.

iPhone (Safari)

1. Open Safari and go to tcradios-new.vercel.app.
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow).
3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
4. Tap Add – TC RADIOS will appear on your home screen like an app.

Desktop (Chrome / Edge)

1. Open tcradios-new.vercel.app on your computer.
2. Click the install icon in the address bar, or use the browser menu.
3. Confirm to install – TC RADIOS opens in its own window.

About TC RADIOS

TC RADIOS is a comprehensive Christian radio platform that brings together uplifting content from Tamil, English, Malayalam, Hindi, Dutch, and Sinhala Christian radio stations. Our mission is to make Christian radio accessible on modern mobile and web platforms.

Built with modern web technologies and optimized for performance, TC RADIOS provides a seamless listening experience whether you're on your Android phone or using the web app on Android, iPhone, or desktop. With optional user accounts, you can sync your favorites, playback history, and ratings across your devices.

Features include global station ratings, playback history tracking, share functionality, lock screen controls, background playback, and stream quality display. All designed to enhance your Christian radio listening experience.
